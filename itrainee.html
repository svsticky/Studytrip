What we offer
iTrainee helps you in your search and gives your career a successful kick-start. How do we do this? With a training programme for SAP, the most widely used business software around the world. After a rigorous selection procedure, you’ll follow an intensive SAP training programme in which you’ll specialise in a functional and/or technical direction while working on your consultancy skills. You’ll then go and work as a trainee at the company that’s best suited to your needs and ambitions. This could be a well-known organisation, like ASML or Heineken, or a consultancy firm like Deloitte or Atos. Is collaboration a success? Then you’ll have found your employer and your contract will be ready and waiting.

iTrainee has already been the golden springboard in hundreds of young people’s careers. Our trainees work on developing new applications for the healthcare sector. They automate entire warehouses with the Internet of Things and robotics. And, they build smart dashboards for managers who want to have the right information available quickly.
