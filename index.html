Istanbul

Info

We will travel to Istanbul, Turkey with a group of 34 students & 2 teachers. We will visit several companies as well as a university. The trip also gives you the opportunity to explore the city by day and night, including cultural activities.

When?

The studytrip will take place from the 12th of April until the 21th of April, which includes 2 days of travelling by plane. This gives us plenty of time to explore the beautiful city of Istanbul.

Travel

Because of the distance, we will have to travel by air! We will depart from Schiphol at 11:20 and will arive in Istanbul at 15:50.

Schedule

The following schedule informs you about sign-up time and compulsory activities for participants right up to the departure date.

  • December 6th

    Sign-ups close

    You have until December 6th to sign up for the studytrip. After this date we'll select the definitive participants with a weighed lottery based on motivation, CV, education level and participation.

  • December 12th

    Announcement of participants

    At the start of December we will announce whether you have made the list!

  • December 17th

    Participants meeting #1

    This meeting everyone will receive the necessary details for the trip. All participants will be required to sign a participation contract.

  • January 1st

    Payment due

    You will have to pay off half by now or you pay the trip in full on January 1st.

  • February 19th

    Payment due

    If you choose to pay half in January now is the time to pay the other half.
