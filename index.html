Hanoi

Info

We will travel to Hanoi, Vietnam with a group of 30 students & 2 teachers. We will visit several companies as well as a university. The trip also gives you the opportunity to explore the city by day and night, including cultural activities.

When?

The studytrip will take place from the 13th of April until the 24th of April, which includes 2 days of travelling by plane. This gives us plenty of time to explore the beautiful city of Hanoi.

Travel

Because we will be going to the other side of world, we will have to travel by air! We will depart from Schiphol at 18:10 and will arive in Hanoi at 15:20.

Schedule

The following schedule informs you about sign-up time and compulsory activities for participants right up to the departure date.

  • December 10th

    Sign-ups close

    You have until December 10th to sign up for the studytrip. After this date we'll select the definitive participants with a weighed lottery based on motivation, CV, education level and participation.

  • December 13th

    Announcement of participants

    At the start of December we will announce whether you have made the list!

  • December 20th

    Participants meeting #1

    This meeting everyone will receive the necessary details for the trip. All participants will be required to sign a participation contract. The meeting will be December 20th, and will start at 5:30PM

  • February 29th

    Payment due

    You will have to pay off the trip by February 29th. It is possible to pay half of the trip earlier on, around the beginning of January

  • March 8th

    Participants meeting #2

    During this meeting, you will get a more indepth look at the local culture and the updated planning. You will also get to know your fellow travelers a bit better. The meeting will be March 8th, and will start at 5:30PM

  • December - September

    Sponsor events

    The dates for these events will be disclosed shortly. Attendence is mandatory.

  • April 13th

    Departure

    We will depart for Hanoi at 18:10 from Schiphol.
